Introduction & Motivation

Digital Accessibility

Breaking barriers for visually impaired users by providing high-quality screen reading capabilities in their native language.

State Language Development

Supporting the National Commission's initiative to digitize and preserve the Kyrgyz language with modern AI tools.

Low-Resource Efficiency

Addressing data scarcity challenges by using Matcha-TTS, which requires less training data than traditional diffusion models.

Real-Time Performance

Targeting real-time synthesis on CPU to ensure the system is deployable on accessible hardware and web platforms.

Context

Kyrgyz Republic (Central Asia)

Impact

Education, Media & Public Services

Tech Stack

Matcha-TTS, PyTorch, ONNX
