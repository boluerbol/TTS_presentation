Training Pipeline

From Raw Audio to Synthesized Speech

Data Preparation

Organize audio and transcriptions into the standard format.

filelist.txt: path/to/audio.wav|text
1
Mel Normalization

Calculate Mel-mean and Mel-std for dataset calibration.

matcha-data-stats
2
Configuration Setup

Modify YAML configs to point to your files and stats.

configs/data/ljspeech.yaml
3
Alignment Learning (MAS)

Monotonic Alignment Search learns character durations.

Auto-learned during training
4
Training Execution

Train the ODE-based flow matching model.

python matcha/train.py experiment=my_config
5
Inference Generation

Generate Mel-spectrograms from text using the trained checkpoint.

synth.py --checkpoint ...
6
