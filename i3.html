Monotonic Alignment

Uses Monotonic Alignment Search (MAS) to learn precise character-to-sound duration mappings, ensuring stable speech tempo without skipping words.

Flow Matching Efficiency

Unlike diffusion models that take hundreds of steps, the CFM Decoder finds the optimal straight path from noise to audio in just ~10 steps.

Real-Time Performance

Designed for low-latency inference on standard CPUs, making it ideal for mobile apps and web integration for the Kyrgyz language.
