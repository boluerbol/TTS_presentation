Core Architecture

Three Technical Pillars of KyrgyzTTS

Conditional Flow Matching

Optimal Transport (OT): Learns the shortest, straightest path to transform noise into data.

Efficiency: Requires significantly fewer ODE solver steps (6–10) compared to diffusion models.

Stability: Direct vector field regression avoids the instability of complex score matching.

Encoder-Decoder Structure

Encoder: Processes input text into phoneme embeddings, capturing linguistic features.

MAS Alignment: Monotonic Alignment Search determines exact duration for each character.

U-Net Decoder: A 1D U-Net backbone that conditions flow matching on text and time steps.

Neural Vocoder

The "Voice Maker": Converts the generated mel-spectrogram into actual .wav audio waveforms.

HiFi-GAN / BigVGAN: State-of-the-art GAN-based vocoders ensuring high fidelity.

Deployment: Optimized for speed to maintain the real-time capability of the full pipeline.
